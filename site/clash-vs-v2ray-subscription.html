Clash vs V2Ray subscription: which one should you use?

Use this page if you are choosing between the Clash YAML and V2Ray Base64 paths and want the fastest way to match your client with the correct format.

Open Clash Guide Open V2Ray Guide Check Live Status

Clash is usually better when

  • Your client expects remote YAML profiles.
  • You are using a Clash-style desktop or Android workflow.
  • You want a profile structure that matches Clash-oriented apps.

V2Ray is usually better when

  • Your app asks for a subscription URL or Base64 subscription.
  • You are on v2rayNG, NekoBox, Hiddify Next, or Shadowrocket-like flows.
  • You want the broader mobile-first compatibility path in this repository.
